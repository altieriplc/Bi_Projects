I just finished a Profit and Loss (P&L) dashboard in Power BI 📊

I transformed a Profit and Loss statement (2019, electronics retail) into a financial reporting dashboard that compares Budget vs. Actual performance month by month.

What was done:

🔹 Star schema data model (dimensions, fact tables, and calendar)

🔹 DAX measures for Revenue, Profit, Margins, and Variance vs. Budget

🔹 Complete P&L statement with accounting subtotals in the correct order, built using a single measure

🔹 Layered layout: KPIs at the top, performance trends in the middle, and detailed financial statements at the bottom

Key insight: the year ended 1.8% below the profit target, with profit declining more than revenue, indicating cost pressure on profit margins.
